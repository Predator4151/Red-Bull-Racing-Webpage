About Us

Red Bull Racing is one of the most successful and innovative teams in Formula 1. Known for their aggressive driving style, groundbreaking engineering, and relentless pursuit of excellence, they have been at the forefront of motorsport for over a decade.

Founded in 2005, the team has made significant strides in the world of Formula 1, with numerous championships and podium finishes. With top-tier drivers and technical brilliance, Red Bull Racing continues to push the limits of what's possible in racing.
